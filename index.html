About Us

Our Commitment to Sustainability

RR Coirs and NeoCocos proudly operate three state-of-the-art coir manufacturing units in India, backed by 20 years of expertise in the coir industry. As naturalists, we are deeply committed to environmental protection, ensuring sustainable opportunities for future generations. Choosing the right substrate is crucial for healthy cultivation, and our coir products provide an eco-friendly solution for greener, more sustainable growth. With cutting-edge technology and a focus on quality, we produce premium coir products that are entirely free from chemicals. Our offerings, including coco peat, coconut husk chips, and compost pith, serve as ideal growing mediums in horticulture, floriculture, and the substrate industry. Widely used for hydroponic crops, nurseries, plant propagation, and bedding plants, our products support a thriving, sustainable future.
